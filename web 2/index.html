Functional Objective

M&E section mainly is for planning and designing. Mechanical, electrical and electronic systems required for water/waste water systems to achieve optimum solution in all aspect of design parameters.
Other than above main objective, M&E section monitor and guide existing water/waste water systems to optimize its operation, energy consumption to make the system efficient by adopting latest technology.

The key activities of the section are;
- Energy management of the NWSDB.
- Provide M&E service solutions related to pumps & motors, water meters & other mechanical fittings.
- Provide vehicle & related services.
