The electrical engineer is overall responsible for all electrical, electronics and communication design and operational activities at RSC (NW), and it covers.

01. Design of electrical control systems, panels, and communication modules for new designs.
02. Design and modification to existing electrical, electronics and communication systems to adopt new technologies to optimize the operational systems.
03. Design, implement and monitor remote operational and level monitoring systems for existing / new systems to optimize its functional activities.
04. Supervise all contract management activities in E &M activities.
05. Monitor and evaluate energy management system at RSC (NW) and optimization (Maintain the data bases)
